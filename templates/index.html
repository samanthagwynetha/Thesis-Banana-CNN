The Problem

Why Automated Detection Matters

Slow Manual Inspection

Traditional disease identification relies on time-consuming visual inspection by trained experts. This delays treatment and increases crop losses.

Error-Prone Diagnosis

Human assessment is subjective and inconsistent, leading to misclassification and delayed or incorrect treatment decisions.

Reduced Yield & Quality

Undetected diseases like Black Sigatoka and Cordana cause significant crop losses worldwide, impacting farmer livelihoods.

The Solution

AI-Powered Disease Classification

Using deep learning with ResNet50 architecture to automatically classify banana leaf conditions into three categories.

Healthy

Normal banana leaf with no visible signs of disease or infection.

Black Sigatoka

Caused by Mycosphaerella fijiensis, appearing as dark streaks and lesions on leaves.

Cordana

Cordana musae infection causing oval brown spots with yellow halos on leaf surfaces.
